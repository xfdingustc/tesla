Waylens Privacy Policy

Waylens, Inc. provides a new intelligent camera system designed to amplify your fun behind the wheel with its high-performance camera, stylish steering wheel-mounted remote, and a wireless adaptor that connects to a vehicle’s computer. The Waylens system records and overlays real-time performance data over stunning HD video to capture every aspect of your drive. The system includes an innovative mobile app, cloud server and automated smart editing software that uses algorithms to pick out the best "Moments" from your hours of aggregated driving footage.

In providing these valuable services, Waylens collects information about you, your car and driving habits. Waylens understands that privacy is important to you so we are providing a plain English summary of each section of the Privacy Policy below. This Privacy Policy explains the details of how we collect, use, share and protect the information we collect from you. The words that are capitalized are defined at the end of this Policy.

This policy only applies to us and to our Agents. We cannot control what other Waylens Users do with any information you share on or through Waylens. Be careful with your own information and what you are willing to share.

This policy applies to information we collect:

  • On Waylens website
  • On the Waylens camera when connected to the Internet wireless or through your computer
  • In e-mail, text and other electronic messages between you and Waylens.
  • Through mobile and desktop applications you download from this Website, which provide dedicated non-browser-based interaction between you and Waylens.

Please read this policy carefully to understand our policies and practices regarding your information and how we will treat it. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use Waylens. By accessing or using Waylens, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Waylens reserves the right to revise and update this policy from time to time. All changes are effective immediately when we post them and apply to all access to and use of Waylens thereafter. If you are a Registered User, we will usually notify you of any changes via email and/or by asking you to review the changes to this Privacy Policy or our Terms of Service the next time you login to Waylens. Our cookies do not, by themselves, contain information that personally identifies you. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit.
